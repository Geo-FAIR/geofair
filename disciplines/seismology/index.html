Seismology

The seismological community has been at the forefront of data standardization and sharing for decades, enabling global networks of seismic observations.

Through the International Federation of Digital Seismograph Networks (FDSN), seismology has developed widely adopted data and metadata standards. Many of these precede the FAIR principles and are now being revisited to better support machine-actionable data, interoperable vocabularies, and modern AI/ML workflows.

GeoFAIR aims to amplify these efforts by connecting researchers to existing standards, repositories, and guidance, and by supporting the evolution of FAIR-aligned seismological vocabularies.
